Critical — Data Reliability Issue

53% of Contacts visitors automatically trigger a data discrepancy event on page load. Partners reporting on client list health are being shown unreliable numbers. This needs to be resolved before Contact Growth reporting can deliver trustworthy insights. Escalation to product and data engineering is required.

Key Insight

Contacts usage is dramatically lower than Email Reporting (3.8% vs 10.8% of active base). The combination of low visibility and unreliable data means partners have little reason to visit today. Fixing data reliability is the prerequisite — without it, any new Contact Growth features will inherit a trust problem.

Expected Impact — Contact Growth Reporting

A dedicated Contact Growth surface would give partners cross-account visibility into list health, growth trends, and engagement quality — the data they need to advise clients on audience strategy.

What "success" looks like after launch
Contacts tab usage exceeds 592 baseline Surfacing list health data in a dedicated view should drive significantly more visits than the current tab.
Data discrepancy events drop to <5% Resolving the reliability issue is the prerequisite for any meaningful adoption growth.
Partners take action on list health insights Drill-downs into underperforming lists, exports for client reporting, and engagement trend analysis.
Data: 2026 YTD (Jan–Feb). Total active customers from BI. Contacts usage from Snowflake. Data discrepancy rate from on-page event tracking.
