Teams Reporting — North Star Framework
Product strategy and measurement framework for cross-account reporting. From the Email Reporting MVP through full multi-channel intelligence — here's the vision, the roadmap, and how we'll measure success.
The Problem
🚫 No cross-account visibility
No consolidated view for strategic decision-making across child accounts.
🔄 Painful manual workaround
Parent users spend significant time on manual reporting — impersonating accounts, exporting CSVs, aggregating by hand.
📈 Blocked enterprise growth
Reporting gaps are a blocker in enterprise sales cycles. Lack of parent-level reporting limits multi-location expansion.
The Opportunity
A unified reporting hub for the entire org
✓ One top-level destination for all aggregate marketing metrics
✓ Visual dashboards with real-time data across every channel
✓ Drill down from org-wide trends to individual account detail
✓ Export a quick snapshot or build custom cross-account reports
✓ Same familiar UI pattern as account-level Reporting
★ North Star Vision

A fully integrated Teams Reporting hub that gives multi-account customers a single destination to view, compare, and act on performance data across all channels (Email, SMS, Social), all accounts, with drill-down, trend analysis, and AI-powered insights — eliminating impersonation entirely.

Goals
🎯 Reduce need to impersonate child accounts
🎯 Reduce # of "My Reports" downloads
🎯 Increase partner reporting usage
🎯 Increase export/report usage
🎯 Increase engagement with reporting insights
🎯 Improve user satisfaction with reporting
Business Impact
Retention & Expansion
The clearest growth lever: not just keeping accounts, but expanding them.
  • → Expansion at renewal: Portfolio visibility makes the case for "get the next 50" — growing contracted accounts, not just retaining them.
  • → Churn prevention: Parent-level reporting fills a gap that competitors already cover. Without it, we risk losing multi-account customers.
  • → ROI justification: Cross-account dashboards give decision-makers proof of value across their portfolio, strengthening renewal conversations.
Activation & Insights
AI-driven insights activate child accounts — the leading indicator of expansion.
  • → Child account activation: Insights surface underperforming accounts, prompting action that drives usage and engagement.
  • → Early expansion signal: Activated child accounts are the strongest predictor of contract growth at renewal.
  • → Multi-channel upsell: As reporting expands to SMS, Social, and Automations, it creates natural packaging opportunities.
