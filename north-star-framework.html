Problem / Opportunity

  • 🚫
    No cross-account visibility
    Parent-level users cannot easily analyze performance across child accounts. There is no consolidated view for strategic decision-making.
  • 🔄
    Painful manual workaround
    Current process: impersonate child accounts, export CSVs, manually aggregate — slow, error-prone, and unscalable.
  • 📈
    Blocked enterprise growth
    Lack of parent-level reporting reduces enterprise adoption and strategic oversight, limiting multi-location expansion.
3

North Star

Objective: Enable parent accounts to view consolidated, actionable reporting across all child accounts for email, contact growth, SMS, social, and automations — reducing impersonation, improving executive visibility, and scaling Teams usage.

"Parent accounts gain actionable, cross-account insights that replace manual work and drive strategic decisions."

% of parent account sessions that lead to ≥1 actionable insight or follow-up action across child accounts

An "actionable insight" is any session where the user performs at least one of:

Drill-down into a campaign or child account
Export via CSV or Report Builder
Identify top-performing lists or campaigns
Resolve bounce / unsubscribe issues
Apply a recommendation from the reporting tool
Share or schedule a report

Business Impact

Gross Adds Impact
Parent-level reporting removes a key blocker in the enterprise sales cycle.
  • → Deal-closing feature: Eliminates impersonation as the "reporting story" in demos, replacing it with a consolidated dashboard that sells itself.
  • → Competitive parity: Closes the gap with HubSpot/Mailchimp who already offer multi-account reporting, removing a reason prospects walk.
  • → New selling motion: "Manage all locations from one dashboard" unlocks net-new parent account acquisition in multi-location verticals.
ASP Growth Impact
Reporting visibility creates natural upsell and larger deal sizes.
  • → Seat expansion: Parent accounts spot underperforming child accounts and add seats/channels to improve results — organic upsell.
  • → Higher contract values: Roll-up reporting proves portfolio-level ROI, justifying premium pricing and longer commitments.
  • → Multi-channel packaging: Phases 3-5 (SMS, Social, Automations) give sales ammunition to bundle more channels into deals, increasing ASP.

Anti-Goals (Explicit Non-Goals)

  • Build a full BI / analytics platform — we aggregate and surface, not replace Looker/Tableau
  • Real-time dashboards — near-real-time (hourly/daily refresh) is sufficient for v1
  • Child-account-level feature parity — parent view is strategic, not operational
  • Custom report builder from scratch — leverage existing Report Builder infrastructure
  • Cross-org benchmarking — comparing between unrelated parent accounts is out of scope
