North Star Statement

Objective: Enable parent accounts to view consolidated, actionable reporting across all child accounts for email, contact growth, SMS, social, and automations — reducing impersonation, improving executive visibility, and scaling Teams usage.

"Parent accounts gain actionable, cross-account insights that replace manual work and drive strategic decisions."
!

Problem / Opportunity

  • 🚫
    No cross-account visibility
    Parent-level users cannot easily analyze performance across child accounts. There is no consolidated view for strategic decision-making.
  • 🔄
    Painful manual workaround
    Current process: impersonate child accounts, export CSVs, manually aggregate — slow, error-prone, and unscalable.
  • 📈
    Blocked enterprise growth
    Lack of parent-level reporting reduces enterprise adoption and strategic oversight, limiting multi-location expansion.
✓

Goals

  • 🔒
    Secure, scalable, role-aware reporting
    Consolidated, multi-account reporting that respects account hierarchies and user permissions.
  • 🔍
    Roll-up + drill-down views
    Provide a framework that supports both portfolio-level roll-ups and campaign/team-level drill-downs.
  • 🏆
    Executive visibility & ROI
    Improve executive visibility, operational efficiency, and return on investment across the Teams product.
2

North Star Metric

% of parent account sessions that lead to ≥1 actionable insight or follow-up action across child accounts Target: 60% at 6 mo

An "actionable insight" is any session where the user performs at least one of:

Drill-down into a campaign or child account
Export via CSV or Report Builder
Identify top-performing lists or campaigns
Resolve bounce / unsubscribe issues
Apply a recommendation from the reporting tool
Share or schedule a report

Supporting Metrics / Drivers

These are the levers that move the North Star Metric. Each driver is measurable and has a concrete target.

Driver What It Tells Us How We Measure Target
Roll-up dashboard adoption Are parent accounts actually using the new reporting? % of parent accounts using roll-up dashboards weekly ≥50% weekly
Drill-down engagement Are users going deeper, or just glancing at top-level data? Avg # of campaign/child drill-downs per parent session ≥3 per session
Report exports Are users taking action on data (sharing, analyzing offline)? # of CSV / Report Builder exports per week 2× baseline
Impersonation reduction Is the new reporting eliminating the biggest pain point? % reduction in impersonation events for reporting tasks ≥40% Δ
Multi-channel coverage Are users getting value beyond email — across channels? % of parent sessions interacting with ≥2 channels ≥30%
User satisfaction Does the experience feel valuable and usable? Pendo / qualitative feedback score (1-10) ≥8 / 10

Alongside channel expansion, we're restructuring how reporting works for Teams — moving from shared legacy surfaces to a dedicated, purpose-built experience.

🚫
Sunsetting
"My Reports" (Legacy)
Shared reporting surfaces that weren't built for multi-account use. Complex, confusing, and not scalable for Teams.
➔
🔧
Building On
Report Builder (New)
The new Report Builder is the foundation. As we expand channels, Teams reporting is built on top of this modern tooling.
➔
🎯

Dedicated Teams Reporting
A clean, purpose-built reporting experience for parent accounts — simple, not complex, designed for multi-account oversight from day one.

Business Impact Alignment

Gross Adds Impact
Parent-level reporting removes a key blocker in the enterprise sales cycle.
  • → Deal-closing feature: Eliminates impersonation as the "reporting story" in demos, replacing it with a consolidated dashboard that sells itself.
  • → Competitive parity: Closes the gap with HubSpot/Mailchimp who already offer multi-account reporting, removing a reason prospects walk.
  • → New selling motion: "Manage all locations from one dashboard" unlocks net-new parent account acquisition in multi-location verticals.
ASP Growth Impact
Reporting visibility creates natural upsell and larger deal sizes.
  • → Seat expansion: Parent accounts spot underperforming child accounts and add seats/channels to improve results — organic upsell.
  • → Higher contract values: Roll-up reporting proves portfolio-level ROI, justifying premium pricing and longer commitments.
  • → Multi-channel packaging: Phases 3-5 (SMS, Social, Automations) give sales ammunition to bundle more channels into deals, increasing ASP.
  • 📈
    Sales & Enterprise Growth
    Consolidated reporting becomes a differentiator in enterprise demos, accelerating multi-location deal velocity and driving gross adds.
  • 💰
    Retention & ROI
    Data-backed insights improve campaign performance across child accounts. Parent accounts see clear ROI, reducing churn and supporting renewal-driven ASP growth.
  • ⚡
    Operational Efficiency
    Reduced impersonation (≥40%), fewer manual CSV exports, fewer support tickets. Time savings reinvested into campaign optimization, improving outcomes that justify expansion.

Anti-Goals (Explicit Non-Goals)

  • Build a full BI / analytics platform — we aggregate and surface, not replace Looker/Tableau
  • Real-time dashboards — near-real-time (hourly/daily refresh) is sufficient for v1
  • Child-account-level feature parity — parent view is strategic, not operational
  • Custom report builder from scratch — leverage existing Report Builder infrastructure
  • Cross-org benchmarking — comparing between unrelated parent accounts is out of scope
