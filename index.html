North Star Statement

Objective: Enable parent accounts to view consolidated, actionable reporting across all child accounts for email, contact growth, social, SMS, and other channels — reducing impersonation, improving executive visibility, and scaling Teams usage.

"Parent accounts gain actionable, cross-account insights that replace manual work and drive strategic decisions."
2

North Star Metric

% of parent account sessions that lead to ≥1 actionable insight or follow-up action across child accounts Target: 60% at 6 mo

An "actionable insight" is any session where the user performs at least one of:

Drill-down into a campaign or child account
Export via CSV or Report Builder
Identify top-performing lists or campaigns
Resolve bounce / unsubscribe issues
Apply a recommendation from the reporting tool
Share or schedule a report

Business Impact Alignment

  • 📈
    Sales & Enterprise Growth
    Stronger multi-location pitches. Teams reporting becomes a differentiator in enterprise deals, driving seat expansion and upsell.
  • 💰
    Retention & ROI
    Data-backed campaign insights improve performance across child accounts. Parent accounts see clear ROI, reducing churn risk.
  • ⚡
    Operational Efficiency
    Reduced impersonation (≥40%), fewer manual CSV exports, fewer support tickets. Estimated time savings: 2-4 hrs/week per parent admin.
6

Anti-Goals (Explicit Non-Goals)

  • Build a full BI / analytics platform — we aggregate and surface, not replace Looker/Tableau
  • Real-time dashboards — near-real-time (hourly/daily refresh) is sufficient for v1
  • Child-account-level feature parity — parent view is strategic, not operational
  • Custom report builder from scratch — leverage existing Report Builder infrastructure
  • Cross-org benchmarking — comparing between unrelated parent accounts is out of scope
